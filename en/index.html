Research Focus

OUR FOCUS

Digital Mental Health & Anhedonia Monitoring

Developing SHAPS, EMA, moving-average, and personalized dynamic-monitoring frameworks to detect changes in depression and anhedonia risk.

Smart Nursing Education & Simulation

Analyzing heterogeneity of learning responses in simulation-based education to build learner-tailored simulation and outcome-evaluation models.

Care-Industry Technology & Service Transformation

Building assessment indices and guidelines for technology adoption in the care industry to support digital transformation of long-term care services.

Clinical Translation & Institutional Adoption

Moving research beyond papers into field validation, intellectual property, international collaboration, teaching modules, and clinical workflows.
