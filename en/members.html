Chia-Ling Fang

Master's Student (co-advised)

Thesis Topic

Factors associated with dynamic changes of anhedonia in nursing students: a prospective study combining ecological momentary assessment and digital monitoring

Summary

Using EMA-SHAPS and digital-monitoring data with nursing students, this study maps within-person dynamics of anhedonia in daily campus life and explores personal, contextual, and behavioral factors with early-warning indicators.

Background

  • Master's program, Department of Nursing, Asia University

Yi-Fei Hung

Thesis Topic

Factors associated with primary-caregiver burden under the post-acute integrated care model for patients after acute stroke

Summary

Focusing on stroke patients in post-acute integrated care, this study examines current caregiver burden, patient functional status, caregiver characteristics, and predictors of high burden.

Background
