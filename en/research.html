Research

Four pillars spanning measurement, monitoring, translation, and innovation — we care not only about model performance, but about how models are understood, adopted, and used by clinicians to improve care decisions.

Research Focus

OUR FOCUS

① Digital Mental Health & Anhedonia Monitoring

Centered on the Snaith-Hamilton Pleasure Scale (SHAPS), ecological momentary assessment (EMA), and moving-average theory, we develop personalized dynamic-monitoring frameworks to detect changes in depression and anhedonia risk. Related work includes SHAPS cut-off values and predictors (clinical vs. school settings), latent class growth analysis of adolescent depressive-symptom trajectories, and the development and validation of a cross-device anhedonia trend visualization platform.

② Smart Nursing Education & Simulation

We analyze heterogeneity of learning responses in simulation-based education, using responder analysis and mixed methods to ask who benefits most from training, and build learner-tailored simulation and outcome-evaluation models. We also develop AI-assisted nursing-training applications such as AI virtual-human-augmented game-based teaching and a generative-AI + retrieval-augmented-generation (RAG) clinical-reasoning learning system.

③ Care-Industry Technology & Service Transformation

In collaboration with the Industrial Development Administration (MOEA) and the Institute for Information Industry, we build assessment indices and guidelines for technology adoption in the care industry, supporting the digital transformation of long-term care so that technology answers real needs in care settings.

④ Clinical Translation & Institutional Adoption

We move research beyond papers into field validation, intellectual property, international collaboration, teaching modules, and clinical workflows. Our translation model is not about putting technology into healthcare — it is about making technology answer care problems.

Our Approach

OUR APPROACH

Measurable

Explainable

Verifiable

Grounded in field data, pre-post designs, longitudinal follow-up, mixed methods, and real-world outcomes.

Scalable

Deliver papers, systems, course modules, IP, partnerships, and practice guidelines.
